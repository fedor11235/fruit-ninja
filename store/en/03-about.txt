Ninja Fruit Slash is a juicy arcade game that tests your reflexes. Fruit flies into the air — slice it with lightning-fast swipes before it falls! Watermelons, oranges, apples, limes and plums burst into halves with splashes of juice.

But stay sharp: bombs fly up along with the fruit. One careless move and the game is over. Drop a fruit and you lose a life — you only have three.

Slice several fruits with a single swipe to score combos with double points. The longer you last, the faster and thicker the fruit flies. Set records and come back to beat them!

The game works on both desktop and mobile: play with your mouse or a single finger. No complicated rules — just open it and play.
